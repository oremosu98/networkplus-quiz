§ 05Pass guarantee

What it is If you study with Pro, follow the Pass Plan, and fail your first attempt at the relevant CompTIA exam, we extend your Pro subscription so you can prepare for the retake without paying more.

5.1 · Who qualifies

  • You held an active Pro subscription for at least 30 consecutive days before your exam date.
  • You completed the Baseline Diagnostic at least once.
  • You followed the Pass Plan with a documented study pattern — at least 60% of recommended sessions completed (visible on your readiness dashboard).
  • You scored below the official CompTIA passing threshold (e.g. 720/900 for Network+ N10-009) on your first attempt of the relevant exam.

5.2 · What you get

Your Pro subscription is extended by at least 60 days at no additional charge so you can prepare for the retake. The exact extension is set during manual review depending on how far you were from passing and how much active study time you had.

5.3 · How to claim

Email support@certanvil.com within 30 days of your unsuccessful attempt with:

  • The email address on your CertAnvil account.
  • A copy of your CompTIA score report (PDF or photo) showing your exam date, attempt number, and final score.
  • The exam name and version (e.g. "Network+ N10-009").

We review claims manually within 7 days of receipt. We don't penalise you for low effort — the eligibility bar is "you tried and it didn't work", not "you nailed every drill".

5.4 · What's not covered

  • Subsequent attempts (the guarantee covers the first attempt only — though we may extend again in genuine special circumstances at our discretion).
  • Exams you didn't actually take. The official CompTIA score report is required.
  • Fraudulent claims (fake score reports, multiple accounts). We reserve the right to refuse and terminate the account.
  • Beta exams or exam codes other than the ones the Pass Plan was authored for. Currently supported: N10-009 (Network+), SY0-701 (Security+). More to come.
