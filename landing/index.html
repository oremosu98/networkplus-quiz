What's a Performance-Based Question (PBQ)?

PBQs are the hands-on parts of the exam: ordering steps, sorting items into categories, matching, reading logs, filling in config. They hit first, before the nerves settle, and most question banks never let you practice the format, so plenty of people meet their first PBQ on exam day. Sim Lab fixes that: it drops you into the real task types with partial-credit scoring and a "why" on every step, so the format is muscle memory before it counts.
