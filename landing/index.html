Can I really study for free? ▼
Yes — Free tier is unlimited days, 20 questions per day, full diagnostic + Pass Plan + streak tracking. Plenty for daily habit-building; Pro upgrades to unlimited when you want to push harder near exam day.
What's a Performance-Based Question (PBQ)? ▼
CompTIA's hands-on exam format — drag/drop, command-line simulation, network topology configuration. Way more weight on the real exam than MCQs (worth ~40% of your score). Most prep tools skip them; CertAnvil includes a CLI sim, topology builder, and ACL builder right in Pro.
How does the pass guarantee work? ▼
Pro users who put in the work — 30+ days active platform use, completed mock exam, maintained streak — and don't pass on first attempt get their Pro access extended free of charge until they do. Submit your Pearson VUE booking confirmation + score report; manual case review within 7 days.
Do I need my own AI / API key? ▼
No. Pro subscription includes the AI cost. Free tier likewise. We pay Claude API; you pay one subscription. Zero "bring your own key" complexity.

Every question is AI-generated fresh, then run through a 7-layer validation pipeline (prompt self-check, exemplar bank reference, AI second-pass, ground-truth tables, programmatic validators, regression audit, escalation tier).
