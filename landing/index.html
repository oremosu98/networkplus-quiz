Objective 1.3 — Network services and use cases

A network administrator needs to allow traffic from VLANs 10, 20, and 30 across a single physical link between two switches. Which protocol should be configured on the connecting interfaces?

A STP (Spanning Tree Protocol)
B 802.1Q
C LACP (Link Aggregation Control Protocol)
D ARP (Address Resolution Protocol)

802.1Q is the IEEE standard for VLAN tagging. It inserts a 4-byte tag into the Ethernet frame header, identifying which VLAN each frame belongs to. A single trunk link carries traffic for multiple VLANs between switches.

How is this different from other cert prep tools?

Most prep tools ship a static question bank and call it done. CertAnvil generates fresh questions from a curated exemplar bank, validates them through a 7-layer pipeline, and adapts to your weak spots in real time. You study what you actually need, not what a content team wrote once in 2019.

Can I really study for free?

Yes. The baseline diagnostic, study plan, and a daily question allowance are free. You bring your own API key, or upgrade to Pro for unlimited questions and server-side generation with zero key management.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gets you the diagnostic, study planning, and limited daily practice. Pro removes the cap, adds the full exam simulator, PBQ drills, AI teacher explanations, the Network Builder, and cloud sync across devices. $9.99/month or $89/year.

What's a PBQ?

Performance-Based Questions. CompTIA uses them on the real exam to test hands-on skills, not just recall. CertAnvil includes CLI simulations, topology drag-and-drop, and multi-select scenarios so you're not blindsided on test day.

How does the pass guarantee work?

Complete the study plan, hit 85% readiness, and take the exam within 14 days of your target date. If you don't pass, we extend your Pro subscription free until you do. No fine print, no hoops.

Are the practice questions up to date?

Questions are generated against the current exam objectives, not pulled from a static bank. The exemplar library is re-calibrated with each study cycle, and the validation pipeline rejects anything that drifts from the active blueprint.

Can I use CertAnvil on my phone?

Yes. The app is a progressive web app optimised for mobile. Install it to your home screen for a native-feeling experience with offline support. Desktop features like the Network Builder recommend a wider screen, but quiz sessions, drills, and analytics work on any device.

How do I know what to study next?

The dashboard computes a composite readiness score across accuracy, coverage, recency, and volume. Weak spots surface automatically, prescriptive drill links route you to the right activity, and a daily plan keeps you on track without guessing.
