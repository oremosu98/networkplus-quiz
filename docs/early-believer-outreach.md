## Maintain the list (do this BEFORE writing the messages)

If there isn't an explicit list yet, build one in 10 min — Notion, Apple Notes, a text file, anything. Format:

```
Name · medium (text/Slack/email/in-person) · how they saw it · date · what they said
```

Example:
```
Alex · text · saw demo at coffee 2026-04-12 · "this is wild, i'd actually use this for security+"
Maria · email · saw screen-share 2026-03-28 · "send me the link when it's ready"
James · in-person · saw on phone 2026-04-22 · just nodded, said "neat"
[Mum, Dad, partner] · in-person · live with them — they've watched the whole journey
```

**Aim for 5-15 names.** If it's smaller than that, that's fine — early-believer momentum compounds even from 3.

## TEMPLATE 4 — Direct ask (close contacts only)

For 2-3 specific people whose endorsement would carry weight (you'll know who they are), an explicit ask:

> Hey — passed Network+ today, 767/900. Built my own AI quiz to study with. Going to post about it on r/CompTIA + X + LinkedIn over the next couple days.
> If you have 30 seconds, would massively help if you could:
> - Like / share whichever post you happen to see
> - Or reply with even one line about your reaction the first time you saw the app
>
> I know it's a small ask but social proof multiplies fast in cert-prep communities, and the early-launch window is when momentum compounds.
> No pressure. Either way, thanks for being an early believer.

(Use this sparingly — only with people you're genuinely close enough to ask. Reciprocity matters.)

## What NOT to do

- **Don't make it transactional.** "Use my code, get 50% off" — there's nothing to sell yet. The cert-mode SaaS pivot is weeks out.
- **Don't BCC a list of 15 people on the same email.** Each one gets a personalised send, even if 80% of the body is identical. Personal sends = personal trust.
- **Don't include the score and pass mark in the SUBJECT** — keep the subject conversational. The score belongs in the opening line.
- **Don't overshare engineering details** — these are friends and family, not Hacker News. The dev-friendly version is for the public posts.
- **Don't send a launch-day email about cert-mode SaaS pricing** — that's weeks 4-6, after Security+ pack lands and the engine refactor proves out. Today is just "I passed, you should know first."

---

## After sending

- Respond to every reply within 24 hours. Replies are organic word-of-mouth catalysts — fumbling them kills momentum.
- If someone shares your post / story externally, screenshot it + save to a "social proof" folder. These are the testimonial seeds for the eventual cert-mode landing page.
- If anyone offers help (intro, share, design feedback) — say YES. Early believers turn into early ambassadors when given a clear next action.
