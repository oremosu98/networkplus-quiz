## 1 — Spec (what + why)

### What
Add **Microsoft AI-900 Azure AI Fundamentals** as the fourth cert in CertAnvil alongside Network+ (N10-009), Security+ (SY0-701), and AZ-900. Pattern A subdomain: **`ai.certanvil.com`** (see §2 decision #2 + §9 open question for the founder lock — the AZ-900 §9 #2 precedent applied to ALL Azure infra certs; AI-900 is a different role family per Microsoft's own taxonomy). Pro-tier gate (anon visitors can hit the landing diagnostic; switching INTO AI-900 in the cert app requires Pro). All cert-aware home/Progress/Analytics/Settings surfaces lift-and-shift unchanged (they read CERT_PACK at runtime; v7.3.0 proved this at 3-cert scale).

### Why
- **Fourth cert validates the multi-vendor multi-track abstraction** — AI-900 is a different role family (AI/data) from AZ-900 (infra) under the same vendor (Microsoft). Proves cert pack scales across role families, not just vendors.
- **AI-900 is a high-volume cert** with strong 2024-2026 demand post-ChatGPT (per VoC: 115 unique Reddit posts in the AI-900 corpus, 88 post-2024; the May 2025 Azure AI Foundry refresh has created a competitor-gap goldmine — most paid practice tests are behind on Foundry coverage).
- **Personal goal**: founder studying AI-900 after Sec+/AZ-900 — same dogfood pattern that built Net+ to 767/900 pass + the AZ-900 exemplar bank to 194 entries from VoC.
- **Competitor differentiator**: VoC §6.4 + §13.6 confirm Tutorial Dojo has missing-question gaps in 2026; Skillcertpro is a likely braindump source we cannot copy from. CertAnvil's pack with current Foundry coverage from PUBLIC MS Learn fills the gap legally.

### Non-goals
- **No new app architecture** — every cert-aware surface (home, sidebar, topbar, Progress, Analytics, Settings, Quiz, Exam, SR Queue, Wrong Bank, Daily Challenge) reads `CERT_PACK` + `CURRENT_CERT` + `TOPIC_DOMAINS` + `DOMAIN_WEIGHTS` at runtime. Adding AI-900 means populating those + wiring Pattern A subdomain detection. Zero render-LOGIC change. The v7.3.0 ship proved this generalises beyond CompTIA to Microsoft; v7.5.0 proves it generalises across role families within Microsoft.
- **No exam-format changes** — AI-900 has 45 Q / 60 min / scaled 1-1000 / pass 700 (per VoC §7 + Microsoft official). The `examQuestionCount` / `examTimeSeconds` / `examPassScore` / `examMaxScore` fields in `CERT_PACK.meta` already handle per-cert exam shape (v4.99.82 made all consumers use `EXAM_PASS_SCORE` from CERT_PACK). Just populate the AI-900 numbers and consumers cert-flex automatically.
- **No new monetization mechanics** — Pro-tier gate behaviour identical to v7.3.0 AZ-900 (visible in switcher, gated at switch time via `tadSwitchCert`).
- **No paid-bank content ingestion** — original exemplar authoring from the public Microsoft AI-900 Skills Measured + public Microsoft Learn modules only. Same legal discipline as Net+/Sec+/AZ-900 banks. **Critically: NOT the Whizlabs cheat sheet PDF the founder attached** (Whizlabs is explicitly on the §10 prohibited list per CLAUDE.md + the AZ-900 plan §10 lock). The Whizlabs PDF stays unread.
- **No Skillcertpro lookalike content** — VoC §13.6 surfaced Skillcertpro as a likely braindump source (multiple commenters report 80% "word-to-word" matches). The cert pack MUST NOT clone Skillcertpro wording; staying clean of even derivative paraphrase from any leaked source is part of the legal discipline.

### Out-of-scope (deferred follow-ups)
- AI-900 Sec+-style Phase 3 retention concept seeding beyond the initial seed list — defer until founder hits 2-3 gaps in real study (the Jason Dion Method but for Microsoft Learn / official MS practice tests).
- AI-900 GT tables (deterministic-fact tables like `GT_PORTS` for Net+) — AI/ML doesn't have a comparable enumerable-facts set; defer until a clear pattern emerges (might end up being Responsible AI principles enum or AI-service-to-workload mapping).
- AI-900 Pattern A diagnostic page on landing — already cert-agnostic since v4.99.62. Just needs cert tile + diagnostic tile additions for AI-900.
- AI-102 / DP-900 / SC-900 future Microsoft fundamentals certs — the Pattern A precedent will support them; not in scope for this ship.
