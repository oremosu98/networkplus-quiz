Exam mode: one clock, free movement, a pace verdict

Practice mode stays exactly as it is. Exam mode is the Pro layer that makes a session feel like the real PBQ section: one countdown for the whole run, flag a round and come back, a review pass before you submit, and a pacing read at the end against an exam-pace target (a tightened sum of each round's estimated time, so the clock bites like the real one).
