My Recommendation

M05 (Solid Silhouette) primary · M03 (Wedge Wordmark) secondary

M05 — Solid Silhouette wins on minimalist principles AND scaling: strongest silhouette at every size from 16px favicon to marketing hero, reads as "anvil" without any decoration. Pairs cleanly with the Inter wordmark. White on dark / dark on light — themes for free.

M03 — Wedge Wordmark as a secondary identity for places where you've already shown the icon (sidebar, app header) — the wordmark itself becomes the brand. Single-character ▾ swap is subtle and memorable.

Hybrid system: M05 silhouette for app icon / favicon / social avatar / sidebar mark · M03 wedge-wordmark for headers / marketing copy / footer / loading screens. Two assets, full coverage, both stay minimalist.

Honorable mentions: M07 (trapezoid) is the most distinctive single-shape; M02 (dot) is the most discrete; M09 (two bars) is the most reductive but needs the wordmark to read as "anvil."
