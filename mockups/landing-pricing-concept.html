Pricing FAQ

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Pro is month-to-month or annual, cancel from your account dashboard. Annual cancellations stop the renewal but keep your Pro access through the period you've already paid for.
What's covered by the pass guarantee?
Pro users who use the platform actively for 30+ days, complete a mock exam, and don't pass on their first attempt at the real CompTIA exam. Submit your Pearson VUE booking + score report; we extend your Pro access until you pass. Manual review.
Do I need my own AI/API key?
No. The Pro subscription covers the AI cost on our side, your monthly fee includes Claude API tokens. Free tier likewise. Zero "bring your own key" complexity.
Is there a refund policy?
14-day money-back if Pro isn't right for you. After that, the pass guarantee is the active "we'll back you up" mechanism. We'd rather over-deliver on a few cases than under-deliver on the brand promise.
