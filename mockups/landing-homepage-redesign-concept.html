What's a Performance-Based Question (PBQ)?+

PBQs are the hands-on bits of the exam: configuring a network topology, typing CLI commands, sorting things into the right place. Worth roughly 40% of your score and most people fail because they didn't practise them. We bake them in: a real CLI sim, a topology builder, and an ACL lab, not a drag-and-drop pretending to be one. They sit in the Pro tier because they're the heaviest features to run.
