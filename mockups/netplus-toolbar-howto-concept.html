Toolbar & how-to

Everything the Full Toolbar held, summoned when you need it. The eight verbs you switch between live on the canvas; the rest live here.
How to build 5 steps · hide once you know this
1Drag. Pick a device from the palette and drop it onto the canvas.
2Wire. Click device A, then device B, to draw a cable between them.
3Configure. Double-click a device to set IPs, VLANs, routing, DHCP & CLI.
4Move & delete. Drag any device to reposition. Select it and press Del to remove.
5Simulate. Use the Ping / ARP / DHCP tools to trace packet flow.
