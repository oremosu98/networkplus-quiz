The exam opens with the part you can't practise anywhere else.

PBQs are the hands-on tasks: order the steps, sort the items, read the output, fix the config. They come first, when nerves are highest, and most question banks never let you touch the format. Plenty of people meet their first one on exam day.

Sim Lab puts you in the real task types with partial-credit scoring and a "why" on every step, so the format is muscle memory before it counts.
