Guest Isolation from HR

Intermediate N10-009 · 4.3 N10-009 · 4.5 Stateless

Guest Wi-Fi clients must reach the internet freely but must NOT reach the internal HR subnet. Classic VLAN isolation pattern.

Guest VLAN10.10.0.0/24
HR Subnet10.20.0.0/24
Internetany
Requirements
1Deny all traffic from Guest VLAN (10.10.0.0/24) to HR Subnet (10.20.0.0/24).
2Allow Guest VLAN to reach the internet on HTTPS (TCP 443) and DNS (UDP 53).
3HR Subnet is not affected by this ACL.
