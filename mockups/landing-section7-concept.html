What's a Performance-Based Question (PBQ)?

PBQs are the hands-on parts of the exam: configuring a topology, typing CLI commands, sorting things into the right place. They're worth roughly 40% of your score, and most people fail because they didn't practise them. We bake them in with a real CLI sim, a topology builder, and an ACL lab, not a drag-and-drop pretending to be one. They sit in Pro because they're the heaviest features to run.

How does the pass guarantee work?

If you're on Pro, put the work in (30+ days actively studying, mock exam done, streak going), and still fail on your first sit, we keep your Pro access on until you pass. Send us the Pearson VUE booking and your score report and we'll sort it within a week.

Do I need my own AI key or anything?

Nope. The AI cost is on us, Free and Pro both. You don't need to set anything up, paste any keys, or know what an API is. Just sign up and study.

Hit "Notify me" on the cert tile and we'll email you the day it goes live: no marketing list, just that one email. Network+ and Security+ are live now; AZ-900, CCNA, AWS SAA, and AZ-104 are next in the queue.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, from your account in two clicks. Monthly plans stop at the end of the current month; annual plans stop renewing but you keep Pro for the rest of the year you paid for. No cancellation form, no "are you sure" nonsense.

How do you keep the questions original?

Every question is generated from scratch, then put through seven separate checks before you see it. The reference set the AI learns from is 320+ exemplars we wrote ourselves from the public exam blueprint. We don't train on or scrape questions from any paid prep company. It's clean, original, and tied directly to the exam objectives.
