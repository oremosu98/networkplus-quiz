Onboarding rollout

How a user lands

When the onboarding switch is on, every launch runs this path, on web and in the App Store app alike. The only platform difference is the logged-out front door. Anyone already activated feels no change at all.

Kill switch
Flip onboarding_enabled to off. Every device reverts on its next load, without a redeploy.
Activation metric
The share of new accounts that move their readiness in session one. People who skip the diagnostic are tracked separately, so a beginner who studies first does not drag the number down. One Supabase query at this scale.
Cert switching fixed
