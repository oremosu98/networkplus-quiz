My Recommendation

M12 (Hammer Head) primary · M14 (Hammer + Anvil Lockup) for marketing

M12 — Hammer Head is the strongest minimalist pick of round 2. Single shape, single color (amber spark — your brand accent), reads at every size including 16px favicon. The hammer head is the "business end" of the tool — most purposeful, most essential. Pairs cleanly with the wordmark.

M14 — Hammer + Anvil Lockup for marketing surfaces where you can afford more narrative density (hero images, social shares, video thumbnails). Tells the full forge story; carries the brand DNA.

Hybrid system: M12 silhouette for app icon / favicon / sidebar mark · M14 lockup for marketing / hero / loading splash. Two assets, both minimalist, full coverage.

Honorable mentions: M16 (tongs) is the most distinctive — nobody else uses tongs, and the hot piece in the jaws metaphors "your cert being forged" beautifully. M11 (hammer profile) is the most universal-recognition. M17 (flame) is the most modern but loses anvil-specific meaning.
